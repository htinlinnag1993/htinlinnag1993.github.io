Hi, I'm Htin Linn

I'm an engineer from New York City.

------------------------------

I build tools, apps and teams. Entrepreneruship is my passion. Data-driven human-centered design is my approach. I make products with the right balance of engineering & aesthetic. Connecting dots has always been my strongest suits.

I'm currently in my junior year studying computer science at Queens College, City University of New York. Data Structures, Theory of Computation and Computer Architecture are my favorite classes. My study in CS helps me think and make decisions better. The idea of thinking everything in bits and making logical decisions always intrigues me.

I have experience developing and designing softwares and games for the web and VR, from simple landing pages to progressive applications. I like working in agile software development team.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably traveling, drawing, taking photographs, reading on a new topic, playing games, kickboxing, or strength-training.

Journey

------------------------------

  • June - August 2016

    Wow Moment @ BotFactory

    During the software & hardware engineering internship at BotFactory, while working on 3D printers & their softwares, with the support of my supervisors, I discovered my strong passionate for Software Engineering.

  • September 2016 - Present

    The Shift in my Pursuit

    My urge to learn more about Computer Science took me to Queens College to begin my pursuit in CS. My background in Electrical Engineering helped me understand CS in a whole new different level. Graduating in December 2018.

  • January - May 2017

    Acceleration Begins

    TTP Residency @ NYC Tech Talent Pipeline. Completed a highly selective program (Acceptance Rate is less than 15%) delivered by the New York Code + Design Academy for Full-Stack Web Development & Soft Skills in a 25-student-team.

  • February - June, 2017

    Engineer at Work

    Software Engineering Intern at Neuromatters. Assisted & developed VR games, integrating Oculus Rift with Brain Computer Interface Technologies (EEG). Designed & built a client-facing data-visualization web player app to analyze brain data.
